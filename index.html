The Greene Halls

Chambers of the Museum
The Greene Virtual Library
A temple of living books, hidden knowledge, and disciplined study — theology, apocrypha, Hermetica, and your own research, ordered beneath one dome.
The Hall of the Watchers
The two hundred who swore the oath on Hermon and fell — their forbidden arts, their offspring, their doom.
The Hall of the Archangels
The holy ones who stand before the Throne — who beheld the ruin below and were sent to bind the fallen.
The Hall of UFOs
A classified wing beneath the museum — a record of what humanity has seen, feared, studied, and never fully explained.
The Ancient Basilica Sanctuary
A sacred, immersive basilica for prayer and reflection — light a candle, pray the rosary, and rest in quiet from any browser, phone, or VR headset.
The Virtual Library, the Hall of UFOs, and the Ancient Basilica Sanctuary are self-contained — they fall back gracefully and run from any static host. For the Watchers and Archangels halls' 3D models and narration to load, open them from a local server (run start-museum.bat) rather than double-clicking the HTML directly.
